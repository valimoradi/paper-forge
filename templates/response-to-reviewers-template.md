## Letter skeleton

Dear Editor,

We thank the editor and the reviewers for their careful reading and
constructive comments. We have revised the manuscript accordingly; changes
are marked in the accompanying annotated copy. Point-by-point responses
follow.

**Reviewer 1**

*Point 1.1.* "<verbatim quote>"

Response: <what was done and why, or evidence-based rebuttal>. The revised
text appears in §<x>, p.<y>: "<short quote of new text>".

*(repeat per point; editors' comments answered in their own section)*

## Tone rules

- Thank once at the top; no per-point gratitude, no sycophancy.
- Rebut with evidence (a result, a source, a computation), never authority.
- If a reviewer misread, clarify the prose and say what was clarified.
- Every claimed change must exist in the revision; verify pointers before
  sending.
